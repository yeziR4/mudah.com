BIOGRAPHY

Born on December 25th, 1991,Hassan Mudashiru Taiwo popularly known as "Angel Mudah", is a multi-talented artist who has captivated audiences worldwide with his unique fusion of Afro beats and rap.Hailing from Nigeria but now based in the United States, the American-Nigerian sensation has carved out a distinct space for himself in the music industry as a rapper, singer, songwriter, and visionary entreprenuer. While initially embarking on a science-based profession,Angel Mudah's insatiable thirst for knowledge led him to earn a Bachelor's degree in Biochemistry.Undeterred by the demands of academia,he continues to balance his passion for music with scholarly pursuits.
